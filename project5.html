Arduino based bluetooth controlled car


Objective
design a car for assisting blind people to find their way and to receive warnings of obstacles.

Work
- Implemented software algorithm in Arduino, including the Bluetooth controlled functions of move, direction change, warn and stop.
- Designed modules of speech, light sensitivity and temperature sensitivity with chips and sensors.
- Welded circuit and linked interfaces of the Arduino Nano with car gadgets such as wheels, motors, sensors and shell.
